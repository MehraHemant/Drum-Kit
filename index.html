    Instructions to play

  • You can play any instrument by clicking on it or by pressing the key that is written over the instrument.
  • Click on the menu button on the right corner of the window, to get more options to play.
  • After clicking on the menu button you'll get three buttons. i.e. repeat, shuffle, and play/stop.
  • When you click on the repeat button and play a tone, It'll run in a loop.
  • To stop the loop, again click on the repeat button when it turns red click on the instrument you want to stop.
  • Shuffle button shuffle the background music and play it in a loop.
  • When you click on shuffle the play button turn to stop button, you can stop background music by clicking this stop button.
  • When you again click on play button it'll play the last background music.
